Account suspended

Your user account has been suspended. This means you cannot log back in to openRailway until an administrator unlocks your account.

Your account could have been suspended by an administrator or by the system automatically.

Your account may have been suspended for a number of reasons:

  • Unusual activity on your account has been detected by the openRailway system and your account has been locked to protect it
  • An administrator has suspended your account, due to a terms of use breach

You need to contact your administrator in order to have your account unlocked.
