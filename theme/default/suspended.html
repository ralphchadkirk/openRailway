Account suspended

Your account has been suspended by a system manager. This means that you cannot be logged in to the openRailway system.

You will need to contact your openRailway installation system manager in order to have your accound unlocked for you. Only a system manager can unlock suspended accounts.

Your account may have been suspended for a number of reasons:

  • Unusual or damaging activity has been detected
  • You have breached the rules of the software
  • Your account has been suspended for further investigation
